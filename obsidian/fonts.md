# Fonts

Websites that offer free fonts as well as font-based tools.

## Resources

- [Google Fonts](https://fonts.google.com/) - Library of around 1000 free licensed font families
- [DaFont](https://www.dafont.com/) - Archive of freely downloadable fonts
- [Use & Modify](https://usemodify.com/) - Personal selection of beautiful, classy, punk, professional typefaces
- [1001 Free Fonts](https://www.1001freefonts.com/) - Collection of thousands of free typefaces
- [Font Squirrel](https://www.fontsquirrel.com/) - High quality, legitimately free fonts
- [Font Fabric](https://www.fontfabric.com/free-fonts/) - Digital type foundry offering retail fonts and custom typography
- [Just Free Fonts](https://justfreefonts.com/) - Hand-curated collection of free fonts for commercial use
- [Tiff](https://winniethemu.github.io/tiff/) - A type diff tool that visually contrasts differences between two fonts
- [TypeKit Practice](https://practice.typekit.com/) - Learn about typography practices
- [Fontjoy](https://fontjoy.com/) - Generate font pairing in one click
- [Golden Ratio Calculator](https://grtcalculator.com/) - Golden Ratio Typography Calculator
- [FontGet](https://www.fontget.com/) - Variety of fonts available to download, sorted neatly with tags
- [FontPair](https://fontpair.co/) - Helps you pair Google Fonts together
- [Font Space](https://www.fontspace.com/) - Designer-centered free font website with quick customizable previews
- [Abstract Fonts](http://www.abstractfonts.com/) - Fonts free for personal and commercial use
- [Free Typography](https://freetypography.com/) - A list of high quality fonts
- [Leon Sans](https://github.com/cmiscm/leonsans/) - A geometric sans-serif typeface made with code
- [Lexend](https://www.lexend.com/) - Variable font empirically shown to improve reading-proficiency
- [Fonts for Apple Platforms](https://developer.apple.com/fonts/) - System fonts for Apple platforms in your apps
- [SFWin](https://github.com/blaisck/sfwin/) - San Francisco Fonts for Windows 10 and non-Apple platforms
- [Font Flipper](https://fontflipper.com/) - Preview 800+ Google Fonts on top of your own designs
- [Fonts Arena](https://fontsarena.com/) - Free curated fonts
- [Befonts](https://befonts.com/) - High quality fonts for free
- [Arabic Fonts](https://arabicfonts.net/) - Arabic fonts available for free
- [FontDrop](https://fontdrop.info) - Simple and easy way to view the contents of font files
- [Open Foundry](https://open-foundry.com) - FREE platform for curated open-source typefaces
- [Glyphter](https://glyphter.com) - Upload your own SVGs and turn them into font files
- [Google Webfonts Helper](https://gwfh.mranftl.com/fonts) - A hassle-free way to self-host Google Fonts
- [Rough Font Awesome](https://djamshed.github.io/rough-awesome-font/dist/) - When RoughJS meets Font Awesome
- [FFonts](https://ffonts.net) - Stylish fonts for free
- [Malayalam Fonts](https://smc.org.in/fonts/) - Malayalam fonts maintained by Swathanthra Malayalam Computing
- [Dev Fonts](https://devfonts.gafi.dev/) - Find and use coding fonts for free
- [Font M](https://fontm.com/) - Free fonts for material coding and design
- [W Fonts](https://www.wfonts.com/) - Download free fonts
- [Modern Fluid Typography](https://modern-fluid-typography.vercel.app/) - Fluid typography editor for responsive typography
- [UrbanFonts](https://www.urbanfonts.com/) - Collection of over 8000 free fonts and dingbats
- [Typespiration](https://typespiration.com/) - Inspirational font combinations and color palettes
- [Fontsource](https://fontsource.org/) - Self-host Open Source fonts in NPM packages
- [FontBolt](https://www.fontbolt.com/) - Discover and generate your favorite fonts from pop culture
- [Fontshare](https://www.fontshare.com/) - Free fonts service from the Indian Type Foundry
